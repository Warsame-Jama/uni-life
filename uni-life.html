University Life!

A university student's life is very challenging and extensive to say the least, it can be broken down into different activites

There are different courses taught among them Computer Science within which there are different units.

This is a list of the various units taught in second year of Computer Science
  • CSC 222- Automata Theory
  • CSC 216- Assembly Language programming
  • CSC 227- Programming Project
  • CSC 228- Web & Services Programming
  • CSC 229- Machine Language Algorithms

The days and times which of these units are taught vary from Monday- Friday, 9:00A.M to 3:45P.M

Each academic year has two semesters:

  1. 1st Semester - September to December
  2. 2nd Semester - January to May

The exams are done at the end of the repsective semesters and amount to 50% of the overall grade while the C.A.T and assignments amount to the other 50%.

At the end of the 4 years of studying, a student graduates with different levels of distinction based of their 4 year grade accumulation

The different levels of distinction are as follows:
  • First Class Honours - 70% & above.
  • Second Upper Honours - 60% - 70%.
  • Second Lower Honours - 50% - 60%.
  • Pass - 40% - 50%.
There are different universities in Kenya among them being Univeristy of Nairobi which is considered to be the most prestigious in East Africa.
